# Project Brief

## Scope statement

This build session is scoped to creating a landing page website for Crystal’s Beverage Catering Service, operated by Crystal Bruce, serving private events across Alabama, Tennessee, Mississippi, and Georgia. The immediate goal is to convert visitors into event quote requests, with event-date availability as the secondary conversion path. The selected implementation stack is Static HTML/CSS/JS. ChatGPT creates the planning/build package zip. The user creates the local repo from the zip. Claude Code TUI completes the local build and populates GitHub. Vercel deploys from GitHub.

## Audience

Hosts/planners for weddings, corporate events, private parties, and special celebrations.

## Business target

2 events per month.

## Launch posture

Public-facing and SEO-ready, but production-sensitive until domain, form backend, assets, and regulated/trust claims are verified.

## Required page

One landing page only.

## User-supplied content

Crystal Bruce has years of beverage service experience and is supported by competent professionals with years of experience in service, planning support, guest care, setup, coordination, and event-day logistics.

## Exclusions

No alcohol-specific, licensing, insurance, certification, testimonial, review, award, pricing, venue approval, guaranteed availability, or guaranteed-outcome claims.
