# Legal and Claims Review

## Allowed user-supplied claims

- Crystal Bruce owns/runs the service.
- Crystal has years of beverage service experience.
- She is supported by professionals with years of experience in service, planning support, guest care, setup, coordination, and event-day logistics.
- Service supports private events, weddings, corporate events, parties, and special celebrations.
- Service area is Alabama, Tennessee, Mississippi, and Georgia by custom quote and availability.

## Prohibited unless verified

Alcohol service, business-provided alcohol, client-provided alcohol, bartender credentials, licensing, insurance, certifications, health/food approvals, reviews, testimonials, awards, venue partnerships, fixed pricing, guaranteed availability, guaranteed outcomes, and “best/top-rated/#1” claims.

## Required disclaimer

Service details, availability, travel, and beverage arrangements are confirmed during consultation.
