# Design Direction

## Style

Upscale, warm, clean, conversion-focused.

## Colors

- Deep charcoal
- Warm cream
- Champagne/gold accent
- Soft taupe/neutrals
- White/off-white cards

## Layout

Mobile-first, large hero, visible CTA, readable service cards, polished owner portrait card, clear form.

## Owner image

Use `website/assets/owner-crystal-bruce.jpeg` in the Meet Crystal section only.

Alt text: Portrait of Crystal Bruce, owner of Crystal’s Beverage Catering Service.

## Accessibility

Semantic HTML, labels for all fields, visible focus states, keyboard-friendly chatbot, sufficient contrast, descriptive alt text.
