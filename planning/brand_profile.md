# Brand Profile

## Public name

Crystal’s Beverage Catering Service

## Positioning

Upscale beverage catering support for private events across Alabama, Tennessee, Mississippi, and Georgia.

## Brand attributes

Polished, warm, professional, organized, hospitable, elevated, service-minded.

## Visual direction

Deep charcoal, warm cream, champagne/gold accent, soft neutrals, generous whitespace, refined typography, rounded cards, soft shadows.

## Wordmark

Use a temporary upscale text wordmark: “Crystal’s Beverage Catering Service.”

## Avoid

Do not use party-heavy, cheap, novelty, or alcohol-forward language. Do not use unverified “best/top-rated/award-winning” language.
