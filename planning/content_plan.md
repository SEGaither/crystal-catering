## FAQ

Q: What types of events do you support?
A: Crystal’s Beverage Catering Service supports private events, weddings, corporate events, parties, and special celebrations.

Q: What areas do you serve?
A: Service is available across Alabama, Tennessee, Mississippi, and Georgia by custom quote and availability.

Q: How do I request a quote?
A: Submit the quote form, email cbruce.dce@gmail.com, or call Crystal at 256-614-2510.

Q: Can I check whether my event date is available?
A: Yes. Include your event date in the quote form or contact Crystal directly.

Q: Can beverage preferences be customized?
A: Beverage preferences and service needs are discussed during consultation so the service can be planned around the event.

Q: Do you list prices online?
A: No. Custom quotes are prepared based on event details, location, service needs, and availability.

Q: What information should I provide?
A: Event date, location, event type, guest count, contact information, and beverage preferences/service needs.

Q: Are all service details guaranteed?
A: No. Service details, availability, and beverage arrangements are confirmed during consultation.
