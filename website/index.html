Meet Crystal

Crystal Bruce brings years of beverage service experience to private events across Alabama, Tennessee, Mississippi, and Georgia. As the owner of Polished Pours with Crystal, she focuses on creating a polished, welcoming beverage experience that feels organized, personal, and appropriate for the occasion.

Crystal is supported by a capable team of service-minded professionals with experience in event service, planning support, guest care, setup, coordination, and event-day logistics. Together, they help hosts create a beverage service experience that feels smooth, prepared, and elevated from start to finish.

Her approach is built around hospitality first. Whether the event is a wedding, corporate gathering, private party, or special celebration, Crystal and her team bring warmth, professionalism, and presence to the guest experience. The goal is simple: make guests feel cared for while helping the host feel supported.

From the first inquiry to the event itself, Polished Pours with Crystal is designed to feel professional, approachable, and refined. The service is about more than beverages. It is part of the atmosphere, flow, and memory of the event.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of events do you support?

Polished Pours with Crystal supports private events, weddings, corporate events, parties, and special celebrations.

What areas do you serve?

Service is available across Alabama, Tennessee, Mississippi, and Georgia by custom quote and availability.

How do I request a quote?

Submit the quote form on this page, email cbruce.dce@gmail.com, or call Crystal at 256-614-2510.

Can I check whether my event date is available?

Yes. Include your event date in the quote form or contact Crystal directly. Date availability is confirmed during consultation.

Can beverage preferences be customized?

Beverage preferences and service needs are discussed during consultation so the service can be planned around the event.

Do you list prices online?

No. Custom quotes are prepared based on event details, location, service needs, and availability.

What information should I provide when requesting a quote?

Event date, location, event type, guest count, contact information, and beverage preferences or service needs.

Are service details and availability guaranteed?

No. Service details, availability, and beverage arrangements are confirmed during consultation.

Do you provide bartender service for host-supplied beverages?

Yes. Polished Pours with Crystal provides bartender labor, setup support, and guest service for private events. Beverages, including alcohol if served, must be supplied by the host or venue and confirmed according to applicable venue, local, and state requirements.
